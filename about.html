Yo!

Hi. My name is Walter and I am an entrepreneur. I am the CEO of Alekto, a Triangle Startup Factory Fall 2012 alumni company. Our team creates online software to help people maximize their credit scores while dealing with debt collectors. This site is where I write about credit reporting, startups, and tinkering with technology.

I am a lifelong problem solver with a passion for improving the human condition. At the age of 12, I developed my first functional software application and I have been at it ever since. I hold a degree in computer science, specializing in distributed and parallel computing. I also hold an MBA, focusing on entrepreneurship and global strategy. My professional past is diverse, including stints at AOL and Microsoft.

This is my first step out of a lengthy communication darkness. I had basically given up on blogging. But after reading "blogging like a hacker", I am newly inspired.
